Key Features

Built to Last

Constructed with 13-ply, void-free Baltic Birch using glued channel joints for maximum strength. The Quad Cab is engineered to withstand the rigors of the road and the studio for a lifetime.

All-in-One Powerhouse

Powered by a 12" Eminence Swamp Thang speaker, known for its powerful, thick, and chunky tone with a tight, percussive feel. The Swamp Thang provides a tight, low response with a big bottom end and well balanced highs.
Key specifications include:

  • Nominal Basket Diameter: 12", 304.8mm
  • Nominal Impedance: 8 ohms
  • Power Rating: 150Wrms
  • Resonance: 52Hz
  • Usable Frequency Range: 70Hz - 5kHz
  • Sensitivity: 101.8dB
It's the perfect core for your all-in-one rig.

Rack Mount Ready

Integrate your favorite preamps, effects, processors, and power amps directly into the Quad Cab. The 4U rack space provides seamless integration, keeping your essential gear within arm's reach. Rear rack mounts are also optionally available.
